written Jun 9th, 2015 2:30 pm

This is an ongoing series where I share gems I think are worth checking out!

Today we will examine a little gem with great power. Eefgilm is a gemfile cleaner that makes the auto generated rails gemfile much easier to read.

From the Eefgilm Github readme:

Eefgilm automatically modifies a ruby gemfile to make them a little easier to read, it does this by alphabetizing the gems, removing all comments, and removing all whitespace including leading and trailing.
